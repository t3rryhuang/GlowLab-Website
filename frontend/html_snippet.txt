Add this to every page where you want the chatbot.

In the <head>, after glowlab.css:
<link rel="stylesheet" href="chatbot.css">

At the end of <body>, after basket.js:
<script src="chatbot.js"></script>

If your backend is not running on http://localhost:3000, set this before chatbot.js:
<script>
  window.GLOWLAB_CHATBOT_API = "https://YOUR_BACKEND_URL";
</script>
<script src="chatbot.js"></script>
